Detailed Description

The Pipeline skeleton.

Pipelining is one of the simplest parallel patterns where data flows through a series of stages (or nodes) and each stage processes the input data in some ways, producing as output a modified version or new data. A pipeline's stage can operate sequentially or in parallel and may or may not have an internal state.
