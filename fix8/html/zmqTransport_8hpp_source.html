36 /*
37  * The manual of 0MQ can be found at : http://zguide.zeromq.org/
38  * You MUST NOT share ØMQ sockets between threads. ØMQ sockets are not
39  * threadsafe. Technically it's possible to do this, but it demands semaphores,
40  * locks, or mutexes. This will make your application slow and fragile. The
41  * only place where it's remotely sane to share sockets between threads are in
42  * language bindings that need to do magic like garbage collection on
43  * sockets.
44  */
